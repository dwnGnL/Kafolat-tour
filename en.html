Tour program

from 1 to 8 September, 2019

Day 1: Meeting at Dushanbe Airport at 09:00.

Excursion in the cozy city of Dushanbe until the evening (excursion in the bazaar, monuments, national museum of Tajikistan and evening walk)

Day 2: Meeting at the hotel at 08:00.

Tour of the Gissar Fortress, Varzob Valley, Fan Mountains and arrival in Iskanderkul. After dinner, overnight.

Day 3: Rest in Iskanderkul.

Excursion to the Sarytag valley and the Fan Niagara waterfall.

Day 4: Sarytag Valley » departure to Alauddin Lake.

Meeting at the hotel at 09:00.

Day 5: Excursion to Alauddin Lake.

Excursion to Mutny Lake. (From the big Alauddin Lake to Lake Piala and then to the Mutny Lake.)

Day 6: Rest in Alauddin lake.

Excursion from Suhrob Valley to Bodhana Valley.

Day 7: Tour of the Zeravshan Valley and arrival in Panjakent.

Excursion to old Panjakent. At the old bazaar, Sarazm, and modern Penjekent.

Day 8:

Returning to Dushanbe.
